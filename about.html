I’m an established electric cycle mechanic based in Edinburgh, also currently working with colleagues on projects in Belgium and Germany.

Our world is changing ... it’s surely time to get back on our bikes, trikes, tandems .. with a little power assistance which makes all the difference.

So many people are buying expensive ebikes which they really don’t need. £2,000 + for a bike is excessive.

I work from the logical and ecological end of the market. So if you already have a bike, why would you want to buy another shiny bike with some volts in it?

Your own bike - whatever shape, form, size, vintage - can be “electrified” with an add-on kit and battery, and I can convert it for you at a fraction of the price of buying another machine which will cost a lot of wasted money down the line in needless maintenance and servicing.

Whatever your present pushbike, I can electrify it for around £400 including parts and labour, and guarantee it for 1 year.

Compare this with the cheapest model you could buy from China on the internet .. and then contact me. I’m here to talk !

Please browse my photo gallery, which will give you an insight into the various crafts which I have electrified over the years.

You can contact me via the website, and I’m also present on facebook .. But there’s nothing wrong with a phone call - for me it’s always the best place to start!
